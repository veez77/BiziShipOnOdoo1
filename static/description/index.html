BiziShip: AI-Powered Freight Quoting

Seamlessly integrate LTL freight quoting inside Odoo

The BiziShip module connects your Odoo instance diretly to a Mega-Search of Brokers quotes, allowing you to fetch live LTL freight quotes instantly from your Sales Orders. It also provides AI-powered intelligence to automatically extract freight details From your Sale DB or external pdf file.

Key Features

  • Live Sales Order Quoting: A dedicated "LTL Freight Details" tab inside your Sales Orders allows you to compute dimensions, automatically calculate LTL Freight Classes, and fetch real-time carrier quotes.
  • Quote Selection & Booking: Compare carriers, transit days, and total charges directly in Odoo. Select your preferred quote and confirm booking with the click of a button.
  • AI is serving you smartly, to optimize your profit.
